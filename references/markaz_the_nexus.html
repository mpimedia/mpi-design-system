Markaz — Brand Identity

The Nexus

Four blades converge toward a single center point — a diamond that represents the markaz. The negative space between the upper blades forms the letter M. The shape communicates convergence, precision, and centrality without being literal. Works as a standalone symbol at favicon size because the diamond anchors the form even when the blades simplify.
